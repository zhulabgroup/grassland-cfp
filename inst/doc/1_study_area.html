CFP spans large geographic (115-124° W longitude, 28-44° N latitude) and climatic gradients (-8.35-20.3 °C temperature, 54.8-3600 mm precipitation).

Calculate climate change in CFP.

path_climate_monthly <- down_climate_monthly()
path_climate_change_cfp <- calc_climate_change_cfp(path = path_climate_monthly)
df_cc_cfp <- summ_cfp_climate_change()

Historical climate observations show considerable warming (temperature increase by 0.026 °C yr-1) and drying (precipitation decrease by -5.45 mm yr-1) from 1980 to 2019.

From 1980 to 2019, the CFP had an overall warming trend of 0.026 ± 0.0000156 °C yr-1 (mean ± std. err.), with 82.2% of the area having significant warming trends; the CFP had an overall drying trend of 5.45 ± 0.00804 mm yr-1 with 21.4% of the area having significant drying trends.
